Dictionaries allow us to store data, and to associate every piece of inputted data with its own unique key. One very integral property of dictionaries is that every value has its own unique key, meaning that you will never have two keys that are the same within a given dictionary!

\n\n

name = {}

2: name[\"brayley\"] = \"awesome\"
